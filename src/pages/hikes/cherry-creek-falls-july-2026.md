# Cherry Creek Falls
*July 23, 2026*

[Washington Trails Association page](https://www.wta.org/go-hiking/hikes/cherry-creek-falls) for this hike.
[All Trails page](https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/washington/cherry-creek-falls-trail) for this hike.

This was a next level hike for my young grandson as it is 5 miles round trip which definitely was approaching his limit as his legs were tired on the way back.
He handled it like a champ and had a fun journey. My son-in-law brought his two dogs as this is a dog friendly hike too.
