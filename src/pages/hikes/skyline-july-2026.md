# Mount Rainier, Paradise - Skyline Trail Loop
*July 14, 2026*

[Washington Trails Association page](https://www.wta.org/go-hiking/hikes/skyline-trail-loop) for this hike.
[All Trails page](https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/washington/skyline-trail) for this hike.

This is the main hiking route for the Paradise side of Mount Rainier with many photo opportunities that attracts a significant amount of hikers and siteseers.
